Why We Built This

  • No More Paperwork: Replaces notebooks and spreadsheets.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Know exactly where returned items are.
  • Faster Customer Refunds: Process credit notes in days instead of weeks.
  • Better Accuracy: Prevents writing mistakes for item names or quantities.

The Old Problems

Slow Processes

Managers had to wait for paper sheets to travel from the warehouse floor to the office.

Lost Information

Returned items and credit requests were hard to find in paper logs.

Where Data is Stored

Data Group What is inside? Why it is useful
Users Logins, names, and access roles. Controls who can perform what action.
Claims Return details, item counts, invoice numbers. Main record of returned products.
Inspections Physical checks, photos, severity checks. Records product condition.
Audit Trail Who modified what data, and when. Shows a full history of edits.
Slide 07

Safety & Security Rules

  • Role Enforcement: Warehouse staff cannot click the approve button.
  • Authorized Login: Every user must log in with their own password.
  • Secure Profile Panel: Users can change passwords easily.
  • Traceability: Every edit is permanently tied to the user who did it.
